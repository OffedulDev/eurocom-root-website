What is Eurocom?

Eurocom is just a fictional ISP I thought of. I started it to have a more hands-on experience learning BGP, OSPF and other routing protocols as well as network designs and much more. At the moment it's very basic but my goal is to increasingly make it more complex and efficient.

👆 fun fact: the picture seen above has been taken in my homelab where eurocom's first edge router is running!
